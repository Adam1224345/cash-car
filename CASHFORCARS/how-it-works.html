The Full Cash For Cars Process

From quote to payment in under 48 hours — simple, transparent and completely free.

01

Get an Instant Quote Online

Simply enter your vehicle registration number and postcode into our secure quote form. Our system cross-references your vehicle details against live UK market and scrap pricing data to generate a guaranteed price in under 60 seconds.

No personal information required at this stage — just your reg and postcode. Your quote is valid for 7 days with absolutely no obligation to accept.

Get Your Quote Now →
02

Accept Your Quote & Book Collection

Happy with the price? Accept online and choose a collection slot that works for you. Our professional drivers cover the entire UK — we'll come to your home, workplace, or any address you choose.

Can't drive the vehicle? No problem. We bring a recovery truck at no cost for non-runners, MOT failures, and damaged cars. All we need is legal access to the vehicle.

03

Get Paid Instantly — Before We Leave

Our driver arrives, verifies the vehicle matches the description provided, and initiates an instant bank transfer directly to your account. The funds clear before the car is moved — guaranteed, every time.

We also take care of all DVLA notification paperwork on your behalf, so you have complete peace of mind once the sale is complete.
