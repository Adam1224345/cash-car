Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked most.

Why sell to Cash For Cars?

We offer top market value based on live UK pricing data, completely free collection anywhere in the UK, and instant bank transfer payment — all with zero hidden fees. Over 15,000 customers have already chosen us for a faster, fairer way to sell.

What vehicles do you buy?

We buy any car, van or vehicle regardless of age, make, model, mileage or condition — including damaged, scrap, salvage, non-runners, MOT failures, and ULEZ non-compliant vehicles across the whole of the UK.

How do you price damaged or scrap cars?

Our pricing engine combines live scrap metal rates, vehicle weight data, salvageable parts value, and current UK auction prices. You always get a fair, transparent price — never a low-ball offer.

How quickly can I get paid?

Payment is transferred to your bank account instantly on collection day — before the vehicle is moved from your property. Most customers receive funds within minutes of handover.

Can you collect a non-runner or car that won't start?

Absolutely. We bring a free tow truck or flatbed to every collection where needed. There's no extra charge for collecting non-runners — it's all included in the service.

What documents do I need to sell my car?

You'll need your V5C logbook (if available), a valid photo ID, and your bank details for payment. We handle all DVLA notification paperwork on your behalf at no cost.
