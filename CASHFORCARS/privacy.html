Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Cash For Cars ("we", "us", "our") is a UK-based vehicle purchasing service. We are committed to protecting your personal data and handling it in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us at privacy@cashforcars.co.uk.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, email address and phone number
  • Postcode and collection address
  • Vehicle registration number and vehicle details
  • Bank account details (for payment processing only)
  • Communications you send to us

We collect this information only when you voluntarily provide it — for example, when requesting a quote, booking a collection or contacting our team.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Generating accurate vehicle valuations
  • Arranging and confirming vehicle collection
  • Processing your payment securely
  • Communicating with you about your sale
  • Complying with our legal obligations

We will never sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers and transmitted using industry-standard TLS encryption. Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only, and we conduct regular security reviews of our systems and processes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Vehicle transaction records are typically retained for 7 years in accordance with UK financial regulations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyse site traffic. Essential cookies are required for the site to function; analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. You can control and disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related enquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact our Data Protection team:

  • Email: privacy@cashforcars.co.uk
  • Hours: Monday–Friday, 8am–6pm

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data rights have been infringed.
