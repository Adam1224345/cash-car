Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before selling your car to Cash For Cars.

How quickly can I sell my car?

You can receive a guaranteed online quote in under 60 seconds. Once accepted, we typically arrange free collection within 24–48 hours at a time that suits you. Payment is transferred to your bank account instantly upon collection — before the vehicle is moved.

Do you buy damaged or non-running cars?

Yes — absolutely. We buy vehicles in any condition including minor cosmetic damage, major accident damage, MOT failures, mechanical faults, flood damage, fire damage, and completely non-running scrap vehicles. Our quotes are based on live market data, so you always get a fair price regardless of condition.

Are there any hidden fees or charges?

None whatsoever. Our collection service is completely free UK-wide. There are no admin fees, no paperwork charges, and no deductions on the day of collection. The price we quote is the price you receive — guaranteed.

How do I receive my payment?

Payment is made via instant bank transfer at the time of collection. Our driver processes the transfer before the vehicle is moved, and funds typically appear in your account within minutes. We do not pay by cheque or cash.

Do I need to clean the car before collection?

No cleaning is required. We do ask that you remove all personal belongings from the vehicle before collection. The condition of the car — inside and out — is already factored into our quote, so there's nothing to prepare.

What documents do I need to provide?

You'll need your V5C logbook (vehicle registration document) and a valid form of photo ID. If you've misplaced your V5C, we can often still proceed — contact our team and we'll advise you on the next steps.

Can I sell a car that still has outstanding finance?

Yes. If your vehicle has an outstanding finance agreement, we can still purchase it. We'll settle the remaining balance directly with your finance provider and pay any positive equity — the difference between the car's value and the outstanding amount — directly to you.

How long is my quote valid for?

Your online quote is guaranteed for 7 days from the date it is issued. After 7 days, market fluctuations may require us to issue a revised quote. We recommend booking your collection as soon as you're happy with the price to lock it in.

Do you buy vans and commercial vehicles as well?

Yes — we buy all types of vehicles including cars, vans, minibuses, and light commercial vehicles. Visit our Sell My Van page for more information on van valuations and pricing.
